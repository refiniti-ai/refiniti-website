THE IOS Chick Data Pipeline & Property CRM

To: Michelle Pauley, MBA — Founder of IOS Chicks, Brokered By eXp Commercial

From: Dustin | Refiniti.ai & Geopoint Data

Date: October 24, 2023

Subject: Streamlined Data Enrichment and Custom Property CRM Buildout

1. Executive Summary

Refiniti.ai and Geopoint Data are pleased to submit this proposal for THE IOS Chick’s data and application buildout. Our objective is to keep "Version One" simple and high-impact: creating a seamless path from your spreadsheet data through specialized enrichment (lot coverage, zoning, and dimensions) into a dedicated app.

This system will allow your team to view properties, filter by specific lot requirements, track notes/visits, and—optionally—place calls without switching platforms. Additionally, by leveraging the app system, as properties sell and ownership records change, we can automatically purge from the system. The architecture is designed for CRM portability, ensuring a straightforward transition from HubSpot to eXp Commercial’s future CRM when you are ready.

2. The Refiniti Approach: "Analysis First"

We have structured this workflow to ensure we aren't putting the "cart before the horse." Per our strategy discussions, lot coverage analysis defines the dataset before any secondary filtering or contact enrichment occurs.

  • Source of Truth: We will utilize your existing spreadsheets as the primary data source; we will not rely on CoStar API per access restrictions.
  • API Enrichment: Integration of Google Maps API and County Assessor APIs to enrich records with satellite data, zoning, and precise lot dimensions.
  • The "Universe" Definition: Lot coverage analysis (e.g., lot coverage %) is run first to define the qualifying "universe" of properties.
  • Buyer-Centric Filtering: We apply your specific criteria (e.g., lot size < 2 acres) to focus on high-intent needs like contractors and 3PL/construction supply.
  • Ownership Research: Utilization of Geopoint Data Open Corporates (or equivalent) for LLC members/addresses and professional skip tracing for owner contact details.
  • Hybrid Deliverable: A clean output of property essentials (Address, APN, Lat/Long) merged with owner names, phones, and emails.
