VSL + Landing Page Strategy

One clear path: Academy traffic (flyer, site, future channels) → Video Sales Letters → Avatar-specific landing pages with trust and CTA.

  • Introduces Passport to Wildlife Academy: what it is, how students participate, and the lifetime reward (e.g., 15% discount) tied to your average cost of sale.
  • Universal across all segments—foundation for biology, anatomy, and ecology education in the classroom.
  • Creates urgency around joining the academy: limited-time value, exclusivity of rewards, and the cost of not engaging students with a structured, reward-based program.
  • Can be kept universal or lightly tailored by channel (e.g., homeschool vs. district).
  • Customized per customer avatar: homeschool moms, museum administrators, K–12 district buyers, and others you prioritize.
  • Each avatar gets a dedicated landing page with the relevant testimonial VSL, benefit copy, and CTA—so messaging matches who they are and how they buy.
  • One landing page per segment (e.g., homeschool, museum, district) with the appropriate VSL mix and copy.
  • Academy link (once provided) will be integrated; flyer and future campaigns can send traffic to the right page by audience.
