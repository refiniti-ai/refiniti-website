Airbnb Cleaning
Expansion Strategy

A performance-based partnership to dominate the South Florida Airbnb cleaning market through operational excellence and automated sales.

The Partnership Model

We are proposing a high-margin pivot for L&L Cleaning. Instead of chasing one-off residential leads, we will build a dedicated Airbnb Cleaning Division.

Performance-Based Growth: Refiniti provides all sales and marketing for the Airbnb line at zero upfront cost. We earn only when you earn, by taking a margin on top of your quoted cleaning rates.

Note: While marketing is performance-based, there is a one-time investment for the operational and financial infrastructure development required to launch this new line of business.

Zero-risk marketing. We only win if we bring you bookings.

Why Airbnb?

  • Higher Margins: Specialized "turnover" cleans command premium pricing.
  • Predictable Schedule: Most cleans occur in the 11 AM – 3 PM window.
  • Recurring Revenue: One property owner can provide 5-10 cleans per month.

Operational & Technical Setup

To ensure L&L Cleaning delivers a 5-star experience every time, we will implement a complete operational "business-in-a-box" for your Airbnb line.

  • • Custom Checklists: Photo-verified tasks for every room.
  • • Staff Training: Specialized training for Airbnb turnover speed and quality.
  • • Inspector Protocol: Training for senior staff to audit and guarantee quality.
  • • Damage/Stock Reporting: Automated alerts for low supplies or guest damage.
  • • Stripe Integration: Automated intake of cleaning fees from clients.
  • • Automated Payroll: System to pay out cleaners on a "per-clean" basis.
  • • Margin Tracking: Real-time visibility into the profitability of every job.
  • • Upfront Payments: Eliminating the "chasing checks" headache.
  • • Dedicated Landing Page: A high-conversion site for the Airbnb line.
  • • Organic Sales Flow: SEO-optimized booking path for Florida hosts.
  • • Lead Capture: Automated follow-ups for property management inquiries.
  • • Market Analysis: Deep dives into specific Florida zip codes.
  • • Scaling Strategy: How to move from 10 doors to 100 doors.
  • • Custom Integrations: Advanced API connections to property management software.
