Low Ticket · Team-Led

Execution-Only Design Support

~$150 – $250

Best when the client just needs it done and budget is tight. Primarily handled by the design team so core strategy capacity stays focused on larger builds.

  • Simple speaker one-sheeter or marketing one-pager.
  • Basic slide deck clean-up (existing content, light design polish).
  • Single collateral pieces (flyer, social graphics, thumbnails, etc.).
  • Minor tweaks to existing branding or assets.
  • Ideal when content is mostly ready and you need professional packaging.
Minimal strategy involvement · great for “can we just get this done?” asks.
Examples you can quote quickly:
  • Speaker needs a one-page overview PDF before a conference.
  • Client has a rough deck and wants it cleaned up for stage.
  • Brand already has a logo but needs a few on-brand social tiles.
Mid Ticket · Hybrid

Speaker Asset Package

$250 – $1,000

Ideal for speakers and creators who need assets that match their brand and talk. Senior strategist sets direction; the design and build team executes and iterates.

  • Speaker one-sheeter design.
  • Slide deck build for talks (6–10 slides, simple but on-brand).
  • Light brand tightening so decks and one-sheeters feel cohesive.
  • 1–2 feedback rounds to make sure it feels “on them,” not generic.
  • Baseline alignment on fonts, colors, and imagery for future assets.
Moderate strategy involvement on direction & messaging · team handles execution.
Examples you can quote quickly:
  • Speaker needs a short talk deck (6–10 slides) built from outline.
  • Coach wants a one-sheeter + slide deck that share the same visual language.
  • Creator needs visuals that match their brand for a signature talk.
High Ticket · Strategy-Led

Brand System & Stack Build

From ~$1,000+ (scoped)

When you want a senior strategist as the first layer of interface—defining the stack, brand rules, and how everything connects so the client can actually scale.

  • Full brand system and visual identity (logo, fonts, colors, spacing, usage rules) — rebrands typically start around $1,000.
  • Speaker / expert “ecosystem” setup: website, press kit, downloadable style guides, etc.
  • Deep integrations between website and CRM (behavioral automations, workflows, opt-in + asset delivery flows).
  • Technical stack selection and implementation guidance so everything runs efficiently.
  • Roadmap-level thinking around campaigns, assets, and follow-up automations.
High strategy involvement · limited capacity · reserved for serious growth plays.
Examples you can quote quickly:
  • Full expert rebrand: logo, colors, typography, spacing, usage rules, and assets.
  • Speaker platform build: site, press kit, downloadable style guide, and CRM-connected downloads.
  • Client wants their site, funnels, and CRM talking to each other with automated asset delivery.

Pricing is approximate and depends on scope, complexity, and timelines. Quote these ranges on calls; confirm final numbers once deliverables and deadlines are set.
